-: E-mail Editor Help :-

  • To and Cc:
    To and Cc boxes allow you to enter the e-mail andesses of the reccepients to whom to message has to be sent. Reccepients will be able to see who else was sent the message by looking at contents of the To and Cc boxes.
  • Subject:
    The Subject box allow you to enter meaningful summary of the message.
  • Message:
    The Message TextArea allows you to enter the text of your message.
